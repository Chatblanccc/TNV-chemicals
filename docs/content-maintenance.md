# Content maintenance

## Products and applications

Edit structured records in `app/site-data.ts`. Do not add a product until its name, category, intended use, benefits, and technical values have an identified company owner. Unknown technical values must remain `To be verified` or equivalent.

## Translation

English strings are the source content. Add or update the matching Chinese entry in `app/i18n.ts` before publishing a new visible phrase. Verify Chinese rendering in the browser; do not trust file encoding alone.

## Technical resources

Publish TDS, SDS, COA, certificates, or reports only after the company supplies the original file, language/market scope, owner, revision, and effective date. Disabled document controls must not imply availability.

## Inquiry workflow

The current interaction prepares a local draft and intentionally does not transmit data. Connect an approved company inbox/API and privacy workflow before changing the draft message to a submission confirmation.
