# SEO and GEO checklist

- Unique title and description per route.
- Canonical URL and language alternates where supported.
- Accurate Open Graph/X card and alt text.
- One clear H1; logical H2/H3 hierarchy.
- Product-to-application and application-to-inquiry internal links.
- Sitemap and robots include every intended public route.
- Retired routes have one-hop HTTP 308 redirects to current public canonical
  destinations; no loops, chains, locale prefixes, or unpublished targets.
- Organization, Product, Article, FAQ, and Breadcrumb structured data only when corresponding visible, verified content exists.
- Company identity, products, applications, quality process, and contact path stated plainly and consistently.
- No keyword stuffing or hidden content.
- Dates, revision owners, and evidence attached to technical claims.
- Generated placeholders and pending facts never represented as verified company evidence.
